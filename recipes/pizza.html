Pizza

Description

This recipe will guide you through on how to make a pizza.

Ingredients

  • 18 to 20 slices pepperoni
  • 12 ounces mozzarella cheese, grated
  • 1/2 cup pizza sauce
  • 16 ounces of pizza dough
  • 1/2 teaspoon kosher salt
  • 1/2 teaspoon ground black pepper
  • 1 teaspoon olive oil

Steps

  1. Preheat the oven
  2. Make the sauce
  3. Roll out the dough
  4. Add the toppings
  5. Cook the pizza
  6. Slice and serve
